Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from our hospitality partners.

What is the minimum order quantity?

Our MOQ varies depending on the product line and customization requirements. For standard collections, the minimum order is 50 units per SKU. Contact our team for tailored solutions for your establishment.

Do you provide product samples?

Yes, we offer complimentary sample sets for qualified hospitality partners. Request your samples through our contact form or speak directly with our sales team to arrange delivery.

What are your production and delivery timelines?

Standard orders are fulfilled within 2-3 weeks from confirmation. For customized or large-volume orders, lead time is typically 4-6 weeks. We ensure timely delivery across all Emirates.

Can you customize products with our hotel branding?

While we focus on our LUMA Heritage brand identity, we offer elegant packaging solutions that can complement your establishment's aesthetic. Our products are designed to elevate any luxury hospitality environment.
