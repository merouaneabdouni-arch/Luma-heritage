Our Story & Philosophy

A heritage of Middle Eastern beauty rituals — transformed into modern luxury skincare, designed for the world's finest hotels, spas, and concept stores.

An Ancestral Beauty Secret

In the heart of the golden dunes of the Arabian Peninsula, where time seems to stand still, a beauty ritual has been passed down from mother to daughter for centuries.

In the homes of Al Ain, in the United Arab Emirates, women carefully prepare skincare treatments inspired by the generous nature of the desert. Sweet dates, purifying cloves, and regenerating aloe vera are blended into precious formulas, applied with gestures that have been transmitted through generations.

These recipes, whispered during evening gatherings and perfected over time, are more than just skincare — they are an invisible bond between the women of yesterday and those of today.

The Birth of LUMA

The Emirates have always held a deep fascination for me. During my travels to Al Ain, between lush oases and fragrant souks, I immersed myself in the beauty traditions that my grandmother herself knew so well.

I remember her skilled hands preparing balms where the sweetness of dates blended with the strength of cloves and the freshness of aloe vera. These memories became an undeniable truth: these ancestral rituals needed to be revived and adapted to the needs of modern women.

Thus, LUMA was born — a brand where every treatment carries the imprint of these traditions while offering a luxurious and sensorial experience, faithful to the heritage it celebrates.

A secret to be shared, a tradition to be honored, a heritage to be continued.
