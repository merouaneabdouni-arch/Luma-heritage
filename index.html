Elixir – The Secret of Dates

For centuries, dates have been treasured across the Middle East for their restorative and nourishing properties. Rich in vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants, this ancestral ingredient is the heart of LUMA Heritage.

We reinterpret this ancient wisdom through modern luxury formulations, creating an elixir that transforms skincare into a ritual of beauty and well-being.
